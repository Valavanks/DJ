SASTRA UNIVERSITY

The Shanmugha Arts, Science, Technology & Research Academy, also known as SASTRA, is a private and deemed university in the town of Thirumalaisamudram, Thanjavur district, Tamil Nadu, India.
SASTRA is ranked by global ranking agencies such as Times Higher Education and QS.
It offers undergraduate, post graduate and doctoral courses in Engineering, Science, Education, Management, Law and the Arts.
